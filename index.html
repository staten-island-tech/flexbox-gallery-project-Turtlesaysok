Sad Ghost

Fetid Rat

One possible enemy that you have
to defeat to get an identified item
(armor or weapon from the Sad Ghost).
This enemy will have fumes around it,
so fighting in close quarters can be
dangerous.

Gnoll Trickster

One possible enemy that you have
to defeat to get an identified item
(armor or weapon from the Sad Ghost).
This enemy will use poisoned darts and
will try to get away from the melee range.

Giant Crab

One possible enemy that you have
to defeat to get an identified item
(armor or weapon from the Sad Ghost).
This enemy will block all non-suprise
attacks and will even block magic

Old Wandmaker

Corpse Dust

Something to collect for the Old
Wandmaker. Will summon wraiths
if you hold on to it.(Old Wandmaker
will give a choice between 2
upgraded wands)

Rot Heart

Something to battle for the Old
Wandmaker. Will release toxic
fumes when attacked. Will drop
a seed when defeated. Wandmaker
wants the seed so dont burn it!(Old
Wandmaker will give a choice between
2 upgraded wands)

Newborn Elemental

Something to battle for the Old
Wandmaker. Has 30 health and
is weak to freezing. Its attacks
will inflict the debuff "Burning".
Also does high damage ranged
and fighting on water is suggested
(Old Wandmaker will give a choice
between 2 upgraded wands)

Caves & Onward

Pickaxe

Something that the troll will give
you no matter the quest. Depending
on the quest, you might have to
mine gold ore or kill a bat with it
(Will upgrade any equipment as
long as they are both identified
and uncursed)

Dark Gold Ore

Something to collect for the troll.
It will take 3 turns to mine one ore
(Will upgrade any equipment as
long as they are both identified
and uncursed)

Dwarf Token

Something to collect for the ambitious imp
to collect these, you must defeat monks
or golems depending on the imp(Will give
a cursed and identified +2 or higher ring)
